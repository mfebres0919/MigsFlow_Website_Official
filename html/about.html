About Me

A little about me

I’m Miguelle,
A self-taught web developer with three years of coding experience. By day, I work as a Manufacturing Engineer at Boeing. But my dream is to eventually transition into full-time web development, a path I fell in love with during my second.

It was during that internship, while working alongside a Human Factors Engineer, that I was introduced to the world of user experience. I started taking courses, learning how websites are built, and realized that everything we interact with online is created by someone. That someone could be me.

Ever since, I’ve been passionate about coding websites that feel meaningful. Websites that help people and make their business feel real and visible.

Why I Do This

Why I Started MigsFlow

MigsFlow is the result of me wanting to combine my love for problem-solving with the satisfaction of helping people bring their ideas to life. I don’t believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. I listen, I ask questions, and then I build something that reflects you. I want your site to feel like a space you’re proud of. Something you can truly own.

What I Do

My Approach

I build everything by hand using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No drag and drop, no templates. That means the sites I create can’t be edited on the back end unless you know how to code. My job is to be the “wizard behind the curtain,” turning your ideas into a site that looks and feels exactly right, so there’s no need for you to worry about the tech side at all.
