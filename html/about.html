About Me

A little about me

I’m Miguelle,
A self-taught web developer with three years of coding experience. By day, I work as a Manufacturing Engineer at Boeing. But my dream is to eventually transition into full-time web development, a path I fell in love with during my second.

It was during that internship, while working alongside a Human Factors Engineer, that I was introduced to the world of user experience. I started taking courses, learning how websites are built, and realized that everything we interact with online is created by someone. That someone could be me.

Ever since, I’ve been passionate about coding websites that feel meaningful. Websites that help people and make their business feel real and visible.
